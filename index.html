Selected Publications

Exploring Kolmogorov-Arnold networks for realistic image sharpness assessment

Shaode Yu, Ze Chen, Zhimu Yang, Jiacheng Gu, Bizu Feng, Qiurui Sun*

Accepted by ICASSP 2025

This paper explores the application of Kolmogorov-Arnold networks for realistic image sharpness assessment. The approach leverages these networks to evaluate image quality in a manner that more closely aligns with human perception of sharpness.
